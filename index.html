Welltory sees HRV.

HRV measures the orchestra. P&S Monitoring identifies each instrument. That's the gap Welltory hasn't been able to cross — until now.

Welltory · Role
The World's ANS-Aware Wellness UI

The Unsolved ANS Gap
HRV Hears the Duet, Not the Instruments

HRV engines cannot separate parasympathetic from sympathetic contribution. Chronic stress, treatment-resistant depression, Long-COVID, hypertension, and burnout all look the same in HRV — but have completely different P&S root causes requiring different interventions.

PhysioPS · Outcome
Branch-Specific Truth at Clinical Scale

P&S Monitoring independently quantifies RFa (parasympathetic) and LFa (sympathetic), exposing alpha-sympathetic withdrawal, PS excess, and beta-sympathetic compensation. These labels feed back into Welltory — turning "high stress" into a treatable ANS diagnosis.

No Channel Conflict · Pure Amplification

Has used Welltory for 18 months. Tracks HRV daily. Consistently shows high stress, poor recovery, and declining energy — even after diet, sleep hygiene improvements, and therapy. Her BP crept from 118/76 to 138/88 over the last year. She's been told it's "just stress." She knows it isn't.

Welltory · Week 1

She sees a card: "Your nervous system pattern may be affecting your blood pressure and energy recovery. A 15-minute ANS test with our clinical partner could reveal the root cause."

Welltory → PhysioPS · Day 8
Sarah accepts. The referral fires.

With one tap, Sarah consents. Welltory sends her anonymized HRV summary, stress trajectory, and risk flags to PhysioPS via the Referral API — processed on Akamai edge in <1ms. A PhysioPS-networked clinic 3 miles from her office confirms an appointment within 24 hours.

PhysioPS · Day 10
15-minute P&S Monitoring session

Sarah arrives. 15 minutes. No needles, no radiation. The CMU/RMU runs four challenge protocols. RFa and LFa are measured independently and simultaneously. Results: Parasympathetic excess at baseline, alpha-sympathetic withdrawal during standing, beta-sympathetic compensation across all challenges — the exact ANS triad that drives wired-but-tired fatigue, orthostatic BP instability, and "mystery" hypertension in post-COVID patients.

PhysioPS · HumanOS · Day 10
HumanOS generates the report in 27 seconds

The .ans file is uploaded to HumanOS. NVIDIA Blackwell on Akamai edge processes it in 27 seconds. The physician gets a 10-slide clinical deck: PS excess pattern, alpha-sym withdrawal, orthostatic index, Long-COVID ANS signature match, and a targeted protocol recommendation: low-dose Midodrine 2.5mg, electrolyte replenishment, and compression therapy. No antidepressants. No "try harder with lifestyle."

Joint · Day 11
PS/S labels push into Welltory

PhysioPS sends the anonymized results back via the ps-results API. Welltory's AI receives: ps_excess: true, alpha_sym_withdrawal: true, pattern: long_covid_ans. Sarah's Today screen upgrades: her Stress score now shows "Autonomic recovery active" instead of just "High stress." Her energy goal shifts to specific ANS-calming protocols. She finally understands her own nervous system.

Welltory · Month 3
Welltory tracks the protocol in real time

Over 90 days, Welltory monitors Sarah's HRV, stress, sleep, energy, and BP daily. Her numbers shift: HRV from 28ms → 46ms, recovery from 38% → 71%, BP from 138/88 → 122/78. Welltory surfaces these improvements weekly, keeping her engaged and anchored to the platform. Her 12-month retention probability moves from 44% to 91%.

PhysioPS · Month 6
Follow-up P&S test confirms normalization

Six months later, Sarah gets her second P&S test. PS excess has resolved. Alpha-sympathetic activity has normalized. Brain perfusion risk is low. Symptom count: 23 → 5. The test confirms what Welltory's telemetry suggested — and triggers another CPT-billable P&S session (~$250). PhysioPS earns, Welltory retains, and Sarah recommends both to every burned-out colleague she has.
